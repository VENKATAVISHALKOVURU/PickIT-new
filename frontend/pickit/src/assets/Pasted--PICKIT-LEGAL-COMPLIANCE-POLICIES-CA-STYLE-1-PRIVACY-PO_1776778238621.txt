🔐 1. PRIVACY POLICY

PickIT Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how PickIT (“the Platform”) collects, uses, and safeguards user information.

1.1 Information Collected

The Platform may collect the following data:

Personal details (Name, Email Address, Phone Number)
Documents uploaded for printing purposes
Transactional and activity-related data
1.2 Purpose of Collection

The collected information is used strictly for:

Processing and managing print requests
Connecting users with registered print shops
Enhancing platform performance and user experience
1.3 Data Usage & Confidentiality
PickIT does not sell, rent, or trade user data to third parties
Uploaded files are used solely for printing-related operations
Files may be automatically deleted within 24–48 hours after processing, unless required for operational purposes
1.4 Data Security

Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect user data. However, users acknowledge that no digital system guarantees absolute security.

1.5 User Rights

Users may:

Request deletion of personal data
Raise privacy-related concerns through official contact channels

💸 3. REFUND POLICY

PickIT Refund Policy

3.1 Eligibility for Refund

Refunds may be initiated under the following circumstances:

The print request is rejected by the shop
The order is not fulfilled
Significant service failure attributable to the platform or shop
3.2 Non-Eligibility

Refunds shall not be applicable in cases where:

Incorrect or unsuitable files are uploaded by the user
The user cancels after printing has commenced
3.3 Refund Process
Refunds may be initiated automatically or upon request
Processing time: 3 to 7 working days, depending on the payment method
